Closed-loop intelligence

The intelligence layer that makes your company legible to AI.

Loop connects messages, documents, code, tickets, and calls into one system that monitors, reasons, and corrects — in real time.

The problem

Your company context is scattered across a dozen silos.

Teams stitch together tools with custom code. No product connects messages, PRs, tickets, docs, and call transcripts into a single intelligence layer that can reason across them all.

The result: decisions are made in the dark. Conflicts surface weeks later. Sprints get lost.

How it works

Four layers turn noise into a closed loop.

Data ingestion

Connects to every tool the company already uses — messages, tickets, PRs, docs, call transcripts — and pulls continuously.

Memory

Every piece of content is embedded and stored in a vector database. Context from all sources becomes retrievable in one query.

Intelligence

Specialized agents monitor for conflicts and anomalies, answer cross-context questions, and generate executable specs.

Interface

A conversational interface where anyone can query the full company context, plus notifications that surface alerts where the team already works.

Example

From open loop to closed loop.

In an open loop, you discover problems weeks later. In a closed loop, the system catches them in real time.

An engineer opens a pull request for a modal-based onboarding flow. Two weeks earlier, a customer said on a recorded call they wanted an inline form. Loop detects the conflict between the pull request and the call transcript, notifies the product manager in real time, and generates a corrected spec the team can act on immediately. The sprint is not lost.
