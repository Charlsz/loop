Loop

The intelligence layer that makes your company legible to AI by default.

Loop connects messages, documents, code, tickets, and calls into one intelligence layer that learns from them, monitors what’s happening, compares it to what should be happening, and adjusts in real time.

The problem

Your company context is scattered across a dozen silos.

Building this today requires brutal integration work, stitching together tools with custom glue code. There is no product that connects messages, PRs, tickets, docs, and call transcripts into a single intelligence layer that can reason across all of them.

The result: decisions are made in an open loop. Conflicts surface weeks later, engineering builds the wrong thing, and teams lose entire sprints.

How it works

Four layers make the company queryable and turn it into a closed loop.

01 · Data ingestion

Connect everything

Connects to the tools the company already uses and captures messages, tickets, pull requests, documents, meeting recordings, and customer interactions continuously.

02 · Memory

Embed and store

Every piece of content is embedded and stored in a vector database so Loop can retrieve relevant context across all sources at once.

03 · Intelligence

Monitor and reason

Specialized agents reason across the full company context, flag when the team is building the wrong thing, answer cross-context questions, and generate executable specs when corrections are needed.

04 · Interface

Surface and act

A conversational interface for the full company context, plus a notification layer that surfaces alerts where the team already works.

Example

From open loop to closed loop.

In an open loop, you make a decision and maybe check the result weeks later. In a closed loop, Loop monitors what is happening, compares it to what should be happening, and catches conflicts in real time.

2 weeks ago · zoom call
A customer says on a recorded call that they want an inline form for onboarding.
today · github
An engineer opens a pull request implementing a modal-based onboarding flow.
loop detects conflict
Loop reasons across the PR and the call transcript, flags that engineering is building the wrong thing, and notifies the product manager in real time.
corrected spec generated
Loop generates an executable spec agents or the team can act on immediately. The sprint is not lost.

Teams that operate this way can cut sprint time and ship much faster by turning their own artifacts into a self-improving loop.
